Unsalted Anchovy

Unless you are catching anchovies yourself you’ll probably have a very hard time finding unsalted anchovies. Most brands package anchovies with a lot of salt.

Unsalted Anchovy is a Green-Green food. Green-Green foods are safe to eat as a regular part of a diet.
